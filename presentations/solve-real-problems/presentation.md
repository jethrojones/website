# Why you're here
## AI is moving fast

The challenge isn't knowing every tool. It's using AI wisely on real problems.

---

# What I do
## Coaching · Creating · Clarifying

Mastermind and mental fitness for school leaders. Books, podcasts, and speaking. Simple solutions to complex problems.

---

# The overwhelm is real
## Stop putting out fires

AI can help you simplify complex problems — if you bring your expertise to the table.

---

# How AI works
## Next-best-piece prediction

Like assembling a puzzle from prior training data. Strong on formulaic work. Weak where deep understanding is required.

---

# Wisdom over tools
## Ethan Mollick

Wisdom in using AI extends beyond technical knowledge. Your expertise lets you catch AI's mistakes.

---

# Don't outsource the learning
## Use AI to enhance, not bypass

If AI does the thinking for students or leaders, nobody grows.

---

# Solve real problems
## Start with empathy

Think about the issue from the perspective of the people impacted — then redefine the problem before you generate solutions.

---

# Three uses for school leaders
## Data · Communication · Voice

Interrogate data safely. Improve communication. Elevate voices that struggle to be heard.

---

# Data
## Interrogate without exposing students

Use secured tools to research handbooks, brainstorm projects, and partner on real problems.

---

# Communication
## Draft with context, finish with judgment

ChatGPT can help draft parent letters and announcements — you still own the truth and the tone.

---

# Voice
## AI as a communication companion

Predictive text and AI drafting can help students who struggle to express themselves say what they mean.
