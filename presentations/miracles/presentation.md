# Big Impact Mixer
## Share the miracles that shaped your impact

We're going to talk about the miracles in your life that led you to live this way and make your big impact.

---

# How to Tell Your Story
## Event · Backstory · Action

Keep it short enough that everyone gets a turn.

---

# Event
## One sentence

The moment — the miracle itself.

---

# Backstory
## One paragraph

Why this moment was meaningful.

---

# Action
## One sentence

The step you took next — and maybe the next miracle it sparked.

---

# Example
## Event

I was removed from my school with just six weeks left in the year.

---

# Example
## Backstory

I had taken that job knowing it would be my last before consulting. I was afraid to step out on my own, but I knew it was soon time. I was still too afraid to do it, though, and I needed a push.

---

# Example
## Action

I turned to Christ to help me through the depression and began relying wholly on Him to make decisions going forward.

---

# The Rule
## Less than one minute

You should be able to tell each little story in under a minute.

---

# Why this matters
## Two purposes

1. Limited time to share — make it count.
2. You always have limited time to make your point. This is practice for powerful snippets.

---

# Your turn
## Share multiple miracles

A powerful story communicates who you are, what miracles you've seen, and how that helps you achieve bigger impact.
