Notes vs. Blog Posts

As of today, I am in a transition point. I have taken plain text notes since 2009.

In early 2024, I decided to make my note-taking app, Obsidian, the engine behind my blog posts. I wanted everything to be in one place, here at jethrojones.com.

So, the notes are notes that I take and occasionally update. They are linked together as you can see from the graph view on the bottom of each post page.

The blog posts are posts that I wrote many years ago, going all the way back to 2006, when I started blogging.

I do not know if I will migrate all my old blog posts over to notes or if they will stay as blog posts. It is all an experiment.
