--- layout: post title: Interesting Things Happen When You Listen date: '2008-02-26T17:48:00.001-08:00' author: jethrojones tags: - internship - administration modified_time: '2008-02-27T06:31:09.646-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3944273018536227154.post-8216355088420892834 blogger_orig_url: https://mrjonesed.blogspot.com/2008/02/interesting-things-happen-when-you.html --- Yesterday, one of the teachers that was having a problem and discussed it with me gave some good feedback to other people. She didn't say anything to me about it, but other people sure did. She came in and talked to me and I sat there and listened, said that I would do what I could, and then followed up with her later in the day and this morning. All I really did was listen and make sure that she knew I cared about resolving the situation. That is important. The hard thing will be making sure that I remember that when I am an administrator.

Have a Good Life.
