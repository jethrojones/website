Colombian food was our theme for the July 2026 [[Fast Sunday Family Feast]].

{% if page.image %} <img src="{{ page.image }}" alt="Colombian family feast platter with arepas, beans, rice, meats, salad, and grilled plantain"> {% endif %}

Our meal plan included:

- Arepas
- Bandeja paisa
- Colombian-style beans
- Hogao, a Colombian Creole sauce
- Carne en polvo, or Colombian-style powdered beef
- Milhojas con arequipe
- Ensalada de aguacate y tomate

![Ensalada de aguacate y tomate with cucumber and red onion](/assets/fsff-july-2026-avocado-tomato-salad.jpg)

We also wanted to make pandewaffles, which a friend from Colombia suggested. But we couldn't find Tucarina, the special flour mix used to make them, so that part of the plan had to wait.

![Milhojas layered with pastry cream and topped with arequipe](/assets/fsff-july-2026-milhojas.jpg)

Here are the recipes we planned for the feast:

- [How to Make Arepas (3 Ingredients!)](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/048435D6-65D4-4E32-BABF-9C0CD7C8006F?locale=en) — Griddled corn cakes made with areparina, water, and salt, then finished in the oven.
- [Bandeja Paisa Recipe (Paisa Platter)](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/007ADD96-BBB9-477E-8F89-9FD0627908CA?locale=en) — A big Colombian platter bringing together beans, rice, carne en polvo, chicharrón, chorizo, a fried egg, plantain, hogao, avocado, and lime.
- [Colombian-Style Beans Recipe](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/4DB69A8E-7545-4C68-B4DA-E4409B1C0B0D?locale=en) — Pinto or cranberry beans slowly cooked with pork hocks, plantain, carrots, tomatoes, aromatics, cumin, and cilantro.
- [Hogao Recipe (Colombian Creole Sauce)](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/1B5D8B62-3A0C-435B-A89E-D261A87CDD8C?locale=en) — A thick, savory tomato-and-scallion sauce seasoned with garlic and cumin.
- [Colombian-Style Powdered Beef (Carne en Polvo)](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/4D7D6D66-F523-443E-BA38-40831DBC16D2?locale=en) — Seasoned flank or skirt steak that is cooked until tender, then ground into a fine, flavorful beef topping.
- [Milhojas con Arequipe Recipe (Colombian Dulce de Leche Pastry)](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/565AA0CE-E77F-4E98-9E69-FC37EAE39D21?locale=en) — Layers of crisp puff pastry filled with pastry cream and arequipe, Colombia's version of dulce de leche.
- [Ensalada de Aguacate y Tomate (Avocado and Tomato Salad)](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/FFAA5671-12B9-4358-BA7C-7991DA361F6D?locale=en) — Fresh tomato, avocado, cucumber, and red onion dressed with lime, vinegar, olive oil, and cilantro.
- [PandeWaffles](https://recipes.crouton.app/recipes/2B35C220-05AA-4042-965D-A3BCFDA52498?locale=en) — Colombian cheese waffles made with queso costeño and Tucarina mix.
