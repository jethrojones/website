Total passive income for 2022: \$56.88

I started month 0 on the 11th, and got \$5.46 in free stock from signing
up with Robinhood. You can use [my
link](https://join.robinhood.com/jethroj-13f477/new-customer-referral-cash-reward)
and we both get free stock!

At the beginning of Month 1, I made income from dividends.

It took me over a month to actually start doing something! I started
investing just \$100 when I opened the account, bought some ATT&T stock
and then waited.

You see, I was afraid to take action. So, on the 23rd of Month 1, I
bought some stock, and sold some calls. I waited six weeks before I took
any real action!

But that started paying off right away. From my initial investment of
\$400, I made 12.79% (\$56.88) by selling calls on a very cheap stock,
BBIG, which has totally tanked since then by the way.

Friendly reminder, I’m not offering any financial advice. I’m just
sharing my personal journey.

Here’s the cool thing. After doing this for six months now, I can’t
believe how long I waited! My return with passive income is now at
55%!!!!
