We’re going to be talking about how to make passive income.  
I have three main reasons why I want to do this:  
1. Empower educators to take control of their lives and livelihoods.  
2. Talk about something people rarely talk about and make it easier for
people to ask questions  
3. Show how it can be done, starting with just \$200.  
My first thing that I did was just \$200. Honestly, you could even start
with nothing. That’s very possible.  
But I started utilizing a strategy called Covered Calls in the stock
market, because there was very little risk. Not zero risk, but very
little.

Learn more about that by taking my course for FREE
[here](https://jethrojones.gumroad.com/l/pifor200).

So, let’s dig into my passive income for month 1 (May 2022) I invested
\$400 and earned \$51.42 in passive income through dividends and
premiums on covered calls. I reinvested the bonus money to keep building
up my nest egg.
