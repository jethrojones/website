I read a hundred books in 2021, and I even finished my [reading
challenge](https://www.goodreads.com/user_challenges/26741390) early.

I learned a lot, even from the fiction books that I read.

I read almost all of these on my kindle, which makes it a little easier
to keep track of how much I'm learning from them.

Using Readwise.io also helps me see my notes and highlights more
frequently. Use [this link to get a free
month](https://readwise.io/i/jethro20).

From my 100 books I read this year, I added 1500 highlights. That's 15
per book!
