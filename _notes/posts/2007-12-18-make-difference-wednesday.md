and wanted to mention it again. My class has gone to great lengths to
set the day aside to help raise rice for the United Nations. They wrote
a proposal to the administration, they planned out a schedule, made
permission slips to teachers, and passes back from the library. I am so
proud of how hard they worked. So, a lot of students will be filtering
into the library to go to [Freerice.com](http://freerice.com/) and see
how much rice we can to donate to the United Nations. The totals for the
