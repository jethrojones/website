**Voice and Choice**

[Learning to Choose, Choosing to Learn](https://amzn.to/3gbBNVy) by Mike
Anderson

**Genius Ideas**

[Pure Genius: Building a Culture of Innovation and Taking 20% Time to
the Next Level by Don Wettrick](https://amzn.to/3AW1UaC)

**School Supported**

[SchoolX: How principals can design a transformative school experience
for students, teachers, parents – and
themselves](https://amzn.to/34j8fOT)

**Student Initiated**

[Empower: What Happens When Students Own Their Learning by John Spencer
and AJ Juliani](https://amzn.to/37UqvjP)

**Student Driven**

[Courage to Grow by Laura Sandefer](https://amzn.to/3z2MctO)

These are a good place to start if you want to learn about each stage in
the student driven learning process.
