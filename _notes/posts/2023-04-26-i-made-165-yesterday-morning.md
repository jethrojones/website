I got burned a few weeks ago, and so I have not been trading as much
lately as I wasn’t able to hit my goal of 1% return. I’d try to sell
contracts but nobody would buy at that price. That’s OK. I’m still
keeping on.

Also, my passive income this month is still in the positive, even if the
stocks are down overall.

I sold 11 calls of \$RUM for \$.15 each at a strike price of \$10.
That’s what my basis is so that’s right where I want to be selling. If
it gets called away, it’s no big deal. I made 1.5% return on this on
this trade.  
  
My basis in \$RUM is \$10.  
  
My Adjusted Basis for \$RUM is \$8.20.
