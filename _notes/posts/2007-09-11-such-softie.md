Today, this seventh grade girl (not my student) was in the hall talking
to some ninth graders. It is "Unique You Day" at our school which
basically means that kids can dress up as whatever makes them unique.
She had a tiara, and theses ninth grade girls were very cliquish. They
made her feel bad for wearing the tiara and didn't have time to talk to
a little 7th grader. It made me sad to see that. Sometimes I am such a
softie.
