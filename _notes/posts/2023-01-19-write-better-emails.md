I’ve been really interested in AI for a long time, and I recently made
something pretty cool. You can check it out at
[writebettr.com](https://writebettr.com).

What you do is send a poorly worded email to better email at jethrojones
dot com and the AI will write a better email for you. Super simple.

Give it a shot at [writebettr.com](https://writebettr.com). The first
one is free.

In time, I’ll develop it to create blog posts, twitter threads, and
emails out of show notes for podcasts. I’ve already been using it for
that for the last little while. It’s pretty
[amazing](https://twitter.com/jethrojones/status/1605612206531436544?s=20&t=96zYY3FCl6pUUPaiavzNhg).
